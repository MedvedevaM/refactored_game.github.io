Lets Play

Description

Genre

Simple prodigy fight game clone with math, listening, translation and other interesting tasks

Technologies

Game has been built in native JavaScript classes and some JQuery

How to play

Win enemies by choosing spells and solving tasks and pass to a next fight, using right/left arrows on your keyboard

If you play only with keyboard, use Tab for switching between buttons/skills, use Enter to click on buttons and submit your answers and use Space to choose a skill, for such tasks as sorting numbers, matching words and collecting mixed words use right/left/up/down arrows to remove number/letter/word, after winning an enemy move to the next enemy using right/left arrows on your keyboard, in order to close the rating list use Escape.
